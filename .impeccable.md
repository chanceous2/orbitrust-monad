# OrbiTrust — Project Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary:** social-commerce sellers in LATAM who sell via Instagram, WhatsApp and DMs, plus their buyers. Low-to-mid technical literacy; they care about looking trustworthy and proving real sales.
- **Secondary:** hackathon judges at Monad Blitz Buenos Aires watching a live, fast pitch. The UI must read as a real product in 10 seconds, not a contract demo.
- **Job to be done:** turn informal completed sales into portable, verifiable reputation. The core primitive is a *completed commercial operation*, not a review.

### Brand Personality
- Three words: **trustworthy, editorial, verifiable.**
- Voice: calm, premium, precise. Spanish-friendly product. No hype, no crypto-bro tone.
- Emotional goal: confidence and legitimacy — "this is proof, not a screenshot."

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Concept: the verifiable ledger of trust.** A premium paper ledger / receipt (comprobante) aesthetic. Every TrustOrder is a receipt that gets *stamped and sealed on-chain*.
- **Theme: light only.** Warm off-white "paper" canvas, warm near-black ink. No pure black/white.
- **Accent:** Monad **violet** as the primary accent (used with discipline, ~10% of visual weight) + a **"verified" green** as the semantic signal for completed / sealed operations.
- **Type:** editorial serif display (**Fraunces**, incl. italic for flourish) + clean grotesque UI sans (**Instrument Sans**) + a refined **mono (IBM Plex Mono)** used *purposefully* for ledger numerals: order numbers, amounts, ratings, scores, hashes (tabular-nums). Mono here is a ledger device, not lazy "tech" shorthand.
- **Signature motifs:** ink **stamps/seals** for "Verified on Monad", `No. 0007` zero-padded numbering, dashed hairline rules, print **registration / crop marks** at sheet corners, and a perforated/scalloped receipt edge on the hero. Tabular mono figures everywhere numbers appear.
- **Anti-references (do NOT do):** dark mode with glowing/neon accents, purple→blue gradients, glassmorphism, gradient text, pill-shaped neon buttons, identical icon-topped card grids, Inter/Roboto, centered-everything layouts. (These are the AI-slop fingerprints we are explicitly avoiding.)

### Design Principles
1. **Ledger, not dashboard.** Lean on receipt/ledger metaphors: ruled lines, numbering, stamps, tabular figures, crop marks.
2. **Restraint with the accent.** Violet and verified-green are rare and earned; paper + ink carry 90% of the surface.
3. **Typographic hierarchy over chrome.** Big editorial serif statements; few sizes, high contrast; small-caps mono eyebrows. Avoid wrapping everything in identical cards.
4. **Numbers are sacred.** Always mono + tabular-nums for ids, amounts, ratings, scores, hashes.
5. **Every "verified" moment is a seal.** Confirmed on-chain actions are celebrated with a stamp, with the tx hash and explorer link.
6. **Light, accessible, AA+.** OKLCH tokens, warm-tinted neutrals, 4.5:1 body contrast, real focus states, reduced-motion safe.

### Technical constraints
- Next.js 14 App Router + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS, wagmi/viem on Monad Testnet (chain 10143). Preserve all contract/wallet logic; redesign is presentation-only.
